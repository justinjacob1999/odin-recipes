S.O.P.P

Description

Simple one pot meal consisting of sauteed sausages,onions,potatoes,and peppers. Tastes delicious and is easy to make in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 6 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 pounds smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green bell peppers, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skilled over medium heat. Place potatoes in the skillet, cover and simmer for around 10 minutes or until the potatoes are slightly browned.
  2. Stir in the sausage, onions, and peppers. Cover and cook for about 5 minutes, or until onions and peppers are to desired tenderness
  3. Serve immediately. If you would like, garnish with fresh mint, thyme, and parseley.
