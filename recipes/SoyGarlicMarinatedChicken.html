Soy Sauce and Garlic Marinated Chicken

Description

This is a very simple chicken recipe that is very popular with my family. The marinade is really simple to make and it takes only around 15 minutes for the entire recipe to be done (not including marination time). I guarantee your family will love it!

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. In a large nonporous bowl, combine the garlic and soy sauce. Mix well.
  2. Add the chicken and turn, making sure every part is evenly and generously coated. Cover and leave in the fridge to marinate for at least an hour.
  3. Preheat a grill for medium to high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Grill the chicken over medium-high heat for 10-15 minutes per side, or until internal temperature is 180 degrees F. Discard remaining marinade.
